Based on the style, I searched for similar encodings and eventually came across [**Symbol Cipher**](https://www.dcode.fr/symbols-ciphers), which includes a variant known as [**Birds on a Wire Cipher**](https://www.dcode.fr/birds-on-a-wire-cipher). 
The positions and number of birds determine letters based on the cipher's grid.
